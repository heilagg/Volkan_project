WHAT WE DO
The Platform

From Waste to
World-Class Materials

CA Energy's integrated circular economy platform is one of Turkey's most advanced industrial complexes — engineered to close the loop on end-of-life tires and deliver premium-grade carbon materials at global scale.

♻

660,000 t/yr Tires Diverted

End-of-life tires that would otherwise burden landfills become the feedstock for high-value industrial materials.

⚗

Advanced Continuous Pyrolysis

Proprietary 455°C continuous reactor technology converts rubber into rCB, syngas, and pyrolysis oil. 94.2% conversion efficiency. 97.8% plant uptime.

◆

rCB → Graphite → Graphene

Vertical integration from recovered carbon black all the way to graphene — a complete advanced carbon materials value chain.

PLATFORM — ca-10.png
Integrated Complex

11 Interconnected Systems.
One Circular Loop.

01

Tire Receiving & Storage

100 ha NFPA-compliant campus. 60,000 t storage buffer. 24/7 automated check-in.

02

Pre-Sorting & Shredding

2,500 TPD inbound capacity. Metal removal. Bulk shredder lines.

03

Continuous Pyrolysis

3 lines at 455°C. 97.8% plant uptime. 24/7 operation.

04

Carbon Upgrading

3 upgrading lines. rCB refining to graphite and graphene. Automated filling & palletizing.

05

Power Generation

215.6 MW from syngas. 4 × 54 MW generators. Grid-connected.
